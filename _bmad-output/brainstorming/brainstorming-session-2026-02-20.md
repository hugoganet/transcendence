## Technique Execution Results

### Phase 1: Role Playing

**Persona 1: Alex (20yo 42 student, crypto-curious but lost)**

- **#1 AI Coach** (VALIDATED + UPGRADED): Adaptive AI Coach that adjusts to each user's background — developer gets code analogies, baker gets supply chain analogies, teenager gets social media analogies. NOT one-size-fits-all.
- **#2 Interactive Learning** (VALIDATED): Learn by doing, not reading. Sandbox simulations, interactive exercises. Like 42's learn-by-doing philosophy.
- **#3 Leaderboards OK** (VALIDATED): Pseudonym-based competition is fine. Healthy competition drives engagement.
- **#4 Study Buddy Matching** (KILLED): Dropped.
- **#5 Daily Streak as Mining** (VALIDATED): Gamification with crypto metaphors — "mining" knowledge tokens, "hash rate" drops if you miss a day.
- **#6 Scam Radar Training** (VALIDATED): Structured modules teaching scam detection patterns, if teachable like other subjects.

**Persona 2: Marie (52yo office manager, non-tech, scared of crypto)**

- **#7 Adaptive Onboarding** (VALIDATED): App asks questions first — background, fears, interests — to build personalized learning path. Diagnoses knowledge AND emotional state.
- **#8 Anti-Crypto-Bro Design** (VALIDATED): Clean, calm, professional tone. No rockets, no moon emojis. Trustworthy teacher vibe.
- **#9 Real-World Anchoring** (VALIDATED + REFINED): Every concept anchored to daily life situations. Only AI Coach gives more personalized explanations on top.
- **#10 Fake Money Sandbox** (VALIDATED): Full simulation environment — fake euros, fake Bitcoin. Buy, sell, learn wallet concepts. Zero risk.
- **#11 Accessibility First** (VALIDATED): Big text, simple words, clear navigation, tooltip jargon explanations. First-class feature.
- **#12 Progress Validation** (VALIDATED): Social proof ("you know more than X% of adults"), shareable certificates.

**User-Added Social Features:**

- **#13 Social Media Login** (USER ADDED): Facebook, Instagram, Google OAuth for easy signup and friend finding.
- **#14 Contact Finder** (USER ADDED): Find phone contacts already on the platform.
- **#15 Social Sharing** (USER ADDED): Share achievements and progress to social media.

### Phase 2: SCAMPER Method

**S — Substitute:**

- **#16 Missions Not Lessons** (VALIDATED + REFINED): Scenario-based learning with multiple teaching frameworks — by doing, scenarios, interactive, etc. Different framework per concept type.
- **#17 Knowledge Tokens** (VALIDATED): XP replaced by crypto-themed tokens. Mine, stake, trade. The gamification IS the education. Potential future real token.
- **#18 Learning Chains** (VALIDATED, needs feasibility): Progress visualized as a blockchain. Each lesson is a block. Keep alongside custom path idea — filter later.
- **#19 Gas Fee Mechanic** (VALIDATED): Mistakes "cost gas." Run out = wait or watch explainer to refill. Teaches gas concept naturally.

**C — Combine:**

- **#20 Market News Interpretation** (VALIDATED + REFINED): NOT trading advice. Show real market news, user interprets it, explains to AI Coach, AI gives feedback. Clear disclaimer. E.g., "Trump declares new Bitcoin tax — what happens to BTC?"
- **#21 Chat + Learning** (KILLED): Dropped.
- **#22 Wallet-as-Profile** (VALIDATED): User profile IS a simulated wallet. Shows Knowledge Tokens, course "portfolio," "transaction history." Profile page secretly teaches wallet concepts.
- **#23 Market Alert Notifications** (VALIDATED): Notifications styled as market alerts that hook into learning. "Bitcoin hit $100k! Quick 2-min lesson on bull markets."

**A — Adapt:**

- **#24 Mastery-Based Progression** (VALIDATED): Khan Academy-style levels: Familiar > Proficient > Mastered. AI Coach adjusts difficulty based on mastery, not completion. If applicable to crypto concepts.
- **#25 TikTok Micro-Lessons** (VALIDATED): 30-second swipeable video cards with clean animations. Mobile-first, bus-ride learning.
- **#26 Codecademy Sandbox** (KILLED): Dropped.

**M — Modify / Magnify:**

- **#27 AI Coach with Memory** (VALIDATED): Remembers everything across sessions. "Last week you struggled with smart contracts. This week's NFT lesson connects to that." True continuity.
- **#28 Social Proof** (VALIDATED): "You understand more about blockchain than 73% of adults." + "342 people completed this today."
- **#29 Live Market Ticker** (VALIDATED): Real-time Bitcoin/Ethereum prices integrated into lessons. Theory meets reality.

**P — Put to Other Uses:**

- **#30 Graduation Guide** (VALIDATED): Clear endpoint — "You're ready for real crypto. Here's your checklist." Platform as bridge to real exchanges.
- **#31 User-Generated Content** (DEFERRED): Not MVP. Advanced users create mini-lessons. Post-launch feature.

**E — Eliminate:**

- **#32 Kill Wall of Text** (DEFERRED): Design principle, not strict MVP enforcement.
- **#33 Jargon Tooltips** (VALIDATED): Every new term gets instant tooltip with plain-language definition + real-world analogy. Toggle "Simple Mode" for all technical terms.

**R — Reverse:**

- **#34 Problem-First Curriculum** (REFRAMED): Marketing angle, not app feature. Start messaging with "why you'd care" not "what is blockchain."
- **#35 Feynman Technique** (STARRED - TOP TIER): User explains concepts to AI Coach to prove mastery. "Explain Bitcoin mining like I'm 5." AI evaluates explanation and spots gaps.

## Session Summary

**Product Vision:** A beginner-friendly, gamified blockchain learning platform where the gamification mechanics themselves teach crypto concepts (tokens, gas, chains, wallets), powered by an adaptive AI Coach that remembers each user and personalizes the learning journey.

**Key Differentiators:**

1. **The platform mechanics ARE the education** — Knowledge Tokens, gas fees, wallet-profiles teach crypto by being crypto
2. **AI Coach with true memory** — adapts to background, remembers across sessions, uses RAG for deep personalization
3. **Feynman technique built in** — prove mastery by teaching the AI Coach
4. **Problem-first, not tech-first** — every concept starts from a real-world situation
5. **Inclusive by design** — serves both the 42 student and the 52yo office manager equally well

**Module Strategy:** 22 points across 16 modules with 8-point safety margin above 14-point minimum.
